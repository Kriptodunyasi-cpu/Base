CoinmarketBase is a next-generation tracking and interaction platform that simplifies the complexities of the crypto world and relieves investors of "lonely portfolio" stress. It doesn't just track data—it connects you with the community behind the numbers.

---

## 📌 Key Features

* **📊 Real-Time Data Tracking:** Seamlessly monitor live price, volume, and change data for the top 300 crypto assets by market cap.
* **📈 Market Pulse:** Instantly catch opportunities and risks with "Top Gainers" and "Top Losers" lists.
* **📑 Advanced Watchlist & Portfolio:** Add your favorite assets to personalized lists and manage your investment performance from a single dashboard.

## 🌟 Our Edge: "Smart Matching & Social Portfolio"

CoinmarketBase goes beyond standard platforms by allowing you to securely connect your **EVM** (Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, etc.) and **Solana (Phantom)** wallets.

1.  **🔍 Portfolio Analysis:** Your asset distribution in connected wallets is analyzed via AI.
2.  **👯 Investment Twin Matching:** Our algorithm matches you with other users who share a similar risk appetite, asset allocation, or investment strategy.
3.  **💬 Secure Messaging:** If you choose, you can connect with your "Portfolio Twin" to exchange strategies and evaluate market trends together.
