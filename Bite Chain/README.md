# 🍴 Bite Chain (BTCN)

**"Taste the Culture, Chain the Flavor"**

**Bite Chain** is a decentralized social responsibility and content platform that merges global culinary heritage with short-form video content and blockchain-based reward mechanisms. It is a digital kitchen where users share national recipes, traditional desserts, and local drinks while being rewarded by the community.

---

## 🌟 Project Vision
Food is the most universal language. **Bite Chain** leverages Web3 technology to bridge this language with a sustainable economy, allowing creators to transform their culinary skills into digital assets. Our mission is to archive disappearing local flavors while providing creators with a transparent income model driven by social impact.

## 🚀 Core Features

* **🌍 Global Culinary Archive:** National dishes, main courses, traditional desserts, and local beverage recipes from every country.
* **🎥 Short-Video Format:** High-engagement, step-by-step cooking videos that make learning intuitive and fast.
* **💎 Micro-Donation System:** Users who try and love a recipe can send small crypto "tips" (donations) directly to the creator.
* **🤝 Social Responsibility:** A platform dedicated to preserving gastronomic heritage and supporting local culinary talent.
* **📊 Proof of Taste (PoT):** An algorithm-based reward system driven by engagement metrics like likes, comments, and profile visits.

## 💰 Economic Model (Tokenomics & Revenue)

Bite Chain is built on a community-first **Buy-Back** and **Reward Distribution** model:

1.  **Ad Revenue:** As the platform scales, revenue generated from advertisements and sponsorships is collected in a central pool.
2.  **Token Buy-Back:** A percentage of the revenue is used to purchase the project’s native token back from the market.
3.  **Creator Payouts:** These tokens are then distributed to content creators based on their performance metrics (likes, comments, and views) to ensure a fair "Share-to-Earn" economy.

## 🛠️ Technical Infrastructure
* **Network:** Optimized for high speed and low gas fees (Base/EVM).
* **Content Storage:** Decentralized storage solutions (IPFS/Arweave) for videos and high-quality images.
* **Smart Contracts:** Automated and transparent donation and reward distribution mechanisms.

## 📈 Roadmap
* **Phase 1:** Brand identity development and launch of Bite Chain community channels.
* **Phase 2:** Launch of the MVP (Minimum Viable Product) and initial recipe uploads.
* **Phase 3:** Integration of the micro-donation and "Like-to-Earn" systems.
* **Phase 4:** Activation of the ad-revenue model and the first Token Buy-Back operation.

## 🤝 Social Impact
Bite Chain is more than just an app; it’s a movement. Every shared recipe contributes to the preservation of cultural heritage. A portion of platform revenues will also be allocated to global food waste prevention projects.
